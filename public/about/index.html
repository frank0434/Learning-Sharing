About me

My name is Jian Liu, also known as Frank. I work for Plant and Food Research as a Data Scientist. I am also doing my master thesis at Lincoln University as a part-time student. The research topic I proposed covers three different subjects: plant, soil and data science. After graduated from an agricultural degree, I picked up data science skills through the working experience and grow my passion in the applications of data science on investigating the interaction between plants and environments.

This blog was created as a documentation site for the master study. Contents may range from tips and tricks in programming tools like R and Python to errors and solutions for specific modelling framework such as APSIM Next Generation.
